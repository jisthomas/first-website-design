Kerala Tourism

Kerala is situated on the southwestern coast of India, a country in South Asia. This tropical paradise enjoys a unique topography with Arabian sea, a part of the Indian Ocean on the west and the Western Ghats on the east. Major cities are Trivandrum, Kochi, Kollam, Thrissur, Calicut.
